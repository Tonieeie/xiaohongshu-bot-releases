### 2. Getting Started

#### Step 1: Login to the App
When you first open the application, you will be greeted by a login screen.
1.  Enter the **Username** and **Password** you created on the website.
2.  These credentials connect you to the cloud server to sync your tasks and analytics.

#### Step 2: Connect Your Xiaohongshu Account
Before the bot can work, it needs permission to act on your behalf.
1.  On the dashboard, if you see a **"Login Now"** or **"Check Auth"** button, click it.
2.  A browser window will open displaying the **First QR Code**.
3.  Open the Xiaohongshu app on your phone and **scan the code**.
4.  **Wait** for the page to redirect. It will automatically jump to the **Creator Center**.
5.  A **Second QR Code** will appear. **Scan this code as well**.
6.  **Wait** for the application to confirm success and close the window.

---

### 3. Using the Bot

#### Topic Growth (Free)
The bot searches for your target keywords (e.g., "Camping") and interacts with relevant posts to build account weight.

1.  **Enter a Topic**: In the main input field, type the subject you want to target.
2.  **Start Mission**: Click the **Start Bot** button.
3.  **Watch it Work**: The "Live Activity" terminal will show the current action. A browser window will open to perform the task.
    > [!WARNING]
    > **Do not close the automated browser window** while the bot is running.

> [!TIP]
> **Need Customization?**
> If you want more personalized comments or custom search parameters, please contact administration.

#### Industry Analysis
The bot scrapes top-performing posts to help you spot viral trends.

1.  **Enter a Topic**: Type your target subject.
2.  **Start Mission**: Click **Start Bot**.
3.  **Result**: The bot views trending posts and uses AI to generate an industry report.

#### Viewing Account and Analytics
The application includes a specialized "Creator Data" dashboard.
*   Navigate to the **Analytics** tab.
*   **Funnel of Truth**: View conversion rates from Exposure -> Views -> Interactions.
*   **Viral Potential**: A score indicating how likely your content is to go viral.

---

### 4. Troubleshooting

> [!TIP]
> **Login Issues?**
> If the bot isn't running successfully or shows a logout notification, click **Log Out** in the bottom left corner and log in again.

*   **Browser won't open**: Ensure **Google Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge** is installed.
*   **Stuck/Frozen**: Close the application completely and restart it.
*   **Connection Error**: Check your internet connection or valid server status.
